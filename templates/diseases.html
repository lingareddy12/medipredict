Breast Cancer Classification

Built a Logistic Regression model to predict the probability of Breast Cancer by considering the tumor parameters like radius,area and texture. Model is trained with 500+ combined reports of healthy peopel and breast cancer patients. The model predicts the outcome with a precision of 94.59%.

Diabetes Prediction

Built a Logistic Regression model to predict the probability of diabetes by considering the Age,Glucose levels and Blood Pressure as major parameters. The model is trained with 700+ combined reports of both healthy people and diabetic patients. The model predicts the outcome with a precision of 77.27%.
